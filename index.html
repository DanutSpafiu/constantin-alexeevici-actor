CONSTANTIN ALEXEEVICI

Actor & Performer

About

Student of Acting at the National University of Theatre and Film "I.L. Caragiale" in Bucharest (Year IV), trained in the rigorous academic spirit of the actor's art.

An actor with broad expressive availability, with experience in classical theater, performance, and student film productions. He maintains a constant interest in complex artistic projects that combine stage performance with music and physical performance.

With skills in percussion (African djembe, Egyptian doumbek), wind instruments (didgeridoo, Armenian duduk), classical and psaltic singing (bass-baritone), pole dance, acrobatics, circus arts, and clowning, Constantin brings a unique versatility to every role.

Based in Bucharest, he is committed to exploring the boundaries of performance art and bringing authenticity to both stage and screen.
