About

Alexandra Morgan is an award-winning actor and writer based in New York City. With over a decade of experience across film, television, and theater, she brings depth and authenticity to every role.

Trained at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London, Alexandra has performed on stages from Broadway to the West End. Her film work has premiered at Sundance, Cannes, and Toronto International Film Festival.

Beyond acting, Alexandra is a passionate writer whose screenplays have been recognized at numerous festivals. She is committed to telling stories that challenge perspectives and illuminate the human experience.

When not on set or stage, she mentors emerging actors and advocates for greater representation in the entertainment industry.
